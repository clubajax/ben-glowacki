Moldrem to complete combat training in June
Updated: January 02, 2013 - 12:04 pm

U.S. Army Pvt. E-2 Aaron J. Moldrem is slated to complete his one station unit training (OSUT) combat training and graduate June 12. He is currently training with Alpha Company Task Force 1/330th Infantry Battalion at Fort Benning, Ga. Moldrem will return home after graduation for a 14-day leave, after which he will leave for and be stationed at Fort Drum in New York state with the 10th Mountain Infantry Division where he will continue training and await orders for deployment. He also spent four weeks at Fort Jackson, S.C. prior to arriving at Fort Benning.
